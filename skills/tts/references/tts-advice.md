# TTS Best Practices

## Text Preparation

- Always use "and" or "or" at end of comma-delimited lists (improves pacing)
- Short clips (<5s) may produce artifacts — pad with brief intro if needed

## ElevenLabs Models

- `eleven_multilingual_v2` — best for expressive, conversational delivery
- ElevenLabs turbo v2.5 does NOT support SSML phoneme tags
- Only Flash v2, Turbo v2, English v1 support SSML
